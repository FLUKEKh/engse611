Hoshino Ai

Introduction

Hoshino Ai is the main character from the anime and manga series Oshi no Ko, created by Aka Akasaka (the author of Kaguya-sama: Love is War) and Mengo Yokoyari (the illustrator). Hoshino Ai is a charming and talented idol with remarkable abilities in various aspects. Her character is complex and deep, making her an interesting and captivating figure in the story. In Oshi no Ko, Hoshino Ai is an idol who captivates people with her charm, including her singing, acting, and various other skills. She is a character with many layers, alancing hard work to succeed as an idol while maintaining a perfect image in the entertainment industry. Despite facing many challenges in both her personal life and career, this idol still manages to attract the attention of her audience and make those around her feel the warmth and love she has to offer

Profile

    Hoshino Ai grew up as an orphan facing a difficult life before receiving the opportunity to enter the entertainment industry. She became a famous idol in the girl group "B Komachi," where she was able to rise to the position of the center of the group after just four years of debuting in the industry. Her increasing popularity demonstrated her singing and dancing abilities, as well as her attractive image.
